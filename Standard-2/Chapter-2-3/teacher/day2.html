Understanding Tax Filing Requirements

Teaching Guide - Day 2 (Learning Lab)

This guide provides instructional support for day 2 activities of Chapter 2.3: Understanding Tax Filing Requirements.

Learning Lab Overview

Day 2 focuses on practical application of concepts learned in Day 1 through hands-on activities.

Lesson Timeline

Time Activity
5 min Review of Day 1 concepts
15 min Activity 1: Financial Decision-Making Simulation
15 min Activity 2: Resource Allocation Exercise
10 min Activity 3: Long-Term Planning Project
5 min Reflection and discussion

Facilitation Notes

  • For Activity 1, monitor groups to ensure they're considering all factors in their decision-making.
  • For Activity 2, encourage students to explain their allocation choices.
  • For Activity 3, help students think realistically about their long-term goals.

Assessment Opportunities

Observe student participation in activities, evaluate completed projects, and assess understanding through group presentations.
