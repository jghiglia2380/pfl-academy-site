Understanding Tax Filing Requirements

Learning Lab

Welcome to Day 2 of Chapter 2.3: Understanding Tax Filing Requirements. Today we will apply the concepts learned in Day 1 through hands-on activities.

Activity 1: Financial Decision-Making Simulation

In this activity, students will work through a simulation that requires them to make financial decisions based on different scenarios.

Activity 2: Resource Allocation Exercise

Students will practice allocating limited resources to meet various financial goals and objectives.

Activity 3: Long-Term Planning Project

This project helps students develop a long-term financial plan based on their personal goals and objectives.

Reflection

Students will reflect on what they've learned and how they can apply these concepts to their own lives.
